Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your review center different from others?

Our review center combines expert instruction, proven methodologies, and personalized support to ensure maximum success rates. We provide comprehensive study materials, regular assessments, and one-on-one coaching when needed.

How long is the review program?

Our standard review program runs for 12-16 weeks, depending on the specific teaching license examination. We also offer intensive crash courses and extended programs based on your preparation needs and timeline.

Do you offer online and in-person classes?

Yes! We offer both online and in-person classes to accommodate different learning preferences and schedules. Our online platform provides the same quality instruction with interactive features and recorded sessions.
